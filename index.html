The Magic Mirror

✨ How to play ✨

1. Conjure: Type a magical item below and cast the spell to conjure your object.

2. Grasp: Hover your hand in front of the camera to grab the magical item.

3. The Aura: Rapidly shake your open hand back and forth to reveal your glowing Fairy aura!

4. The Grand Finale: Close your hand into a tight fist to cast the ultimate spell! The mirror will take a magical portrait and permanently transform you and your room into a majestic Fairy woodland.
